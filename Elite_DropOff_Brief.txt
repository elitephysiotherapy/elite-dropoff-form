1. WHAT WE ARE BUILDING

Elite Physiotherapy currently spends 5–7 hours per week manually managing patient drop-off data — pulling it from Cliniko, entering it into Google Sheets, distributing it to physios, and producing weekly and monthly trend reports. This system automates the entire workflow.

The system will run automatically every morning at 7am and will:

	•	Pull all cancellations, Did Not Attends (DNAs), and did-not-rebook patients from the previous 24 hours directly from Cliniko via its API
	•	Categorise each patient by body area, pathology, session number, and physio
	•	Write all data to a central Google Sheet with weekly tabs and a live monthly summary
	•	Send each physio a private Slack message listing only their own patients
	•	Send the reception team a Slack message with their reactivation call list for the day
	•	Send Marty a CEO summary Slack message with clinic-wide drop-off stats
	•	Provide each physio a link to a simple web form to classify each drop-off as clinical or non-clinical
	•	Calculate the Initial Assessment Rebook Rate automatically, per physio and clinic-wide

KEY OUTCOME
Sinead's 2-3 hour Monday task: ELIMINATED
Admin reactivation calls: same day instead of following Monday
Marty's weekly/monthly reporting: automated
Patient data stays within Elite's own systems — no AI reads patient data during daily operation

2. HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS

2.1 Daily Automated Flow

Every morning at 7am, the script runs automatically on Marty's MacBook Air (M3). The sequence is:

	•	Connect to Cliniko API and pull all appointments from the previous 24 hours that were cancelled, DNA'd, or not rebooked
	•	For each patient: retrieve their full appointment history to determine which session number they dropped off at (anchored to their most recent Initial Assessment)
	•	Retrieve the presenting complaint and body area from the patient's clinical notes in Cliniko
	•	Categorise the body area into Elite's standard categories (see Section 4)
	•	Write all data as new rows into the current week's tab in the Google Sheet
	•	Update the live monthly summary tab automatically
	•	Recalculate the Initial Assessment Rebook Rate for each physio and the clinic as a whole
	•	Send individual Slack DMs to each physio with their patients only
	•	Send the reception Slack channel their reactivation call list
	•	Send Marty a CEO summary Slack DM

7. INITIAL ASSESSMENT REBOOK RATE

This is one of Elite's most important clinical performance metrics. The system will calculate it automatically.

Definition
A patient counts as REBOOKED only if they attended a follow-up session — not just if they booked one.

Calculation
IA Rebook Rate = (Number of IA patients who attended at least one follow-up) divided by (Total number of Initial Assessments performed) expressed as a percentage.

Example: 100 Initial Assessments in a month. 20 patients did not attend a follow-up. IA Rebook Rate = 80%.

Reporting
	•	Calculated per physio and clinic-wide
	•	Reported in Marty's daily CEO Slack DM (month-to-date figure)
	•	Full breakdown in the IA Rebook Rate tab in Google Sheets
	•	Monthly figure locked at end of each month for historical comparison

8. SESSION COUNT LOGIC

Correctly identifying which session a patient dropped off at is critical for trend analysis. Many patients attend for multiple separate complaints over several years. The rule is:

SESSION COUNT RULE
Find the patient's most recent Initial Assessment date.
Count all appointments from that date forward — including the Initial Assessment itself.
That total = the session number the patient dropped off at.

Example: Patient had an IA in 2023 (old episode). New IA in March 2026.
Two follow-ups in April 2026, then cancelled. Session count = 3.
The 2023 episode is ignored entirely.
